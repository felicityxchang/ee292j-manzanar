About the world

This 3D environment is inspired by the watercolor paintings of Kango Takamura, created during his incarceration at Manzanar in 1944.

Scenes are informed by historical photographs, personal letters, and oral testimonies from the Manzanar National Historic Site archives.

Built in Marble, published in PlayCanvas.
